About TASI 2026

India's Foremost
Trust & Safety Convening

The Trust and Safety India Festival (TASI) is India's first national convening focused on trust and safety, led by civil society. TASI creates a rare, collaborative space for dialogue between government, industry, academia, and civil society.

Convened by the Centre for Social Research (CSR) and The Trust & Safety Forum, TASI is recognised as a pre-summit event for India's AI Impact Summit and underscores India's growing role in shaping global conversations on technology governance, ethics, and digital safety.

Structure

Convening Format

TASI 2026 is designed as a dynamic, multi-layered convening integrating high-level policy dialogue with practical, implementation-focused engagement. Moving from vision to execution.

3 Strategic Conference Stages

01. Keynote Addresses

Vision-setting speeches from global leaders shaping AI governance and digital trust establishing the strategic direction.

02. High Level Panels

Multi-stakeholder exchanges on trust, safety and platform accountability encouraging shared problem solving.

03. Fireside Chats

Intimate moderated conversations exploring practical challenges and forward-looking solutions.

04. Policy Roundtables

Closed-door, invitation-only discussions fostering candid dialogue to advance regulatory convergence.

05. Industry Spotlights

Curated presentations highlighting emerging safety tools and responsible innovation models.

06. Interactive Workshops

Hands-on sessions examining tools and case studies where participants engage directly with best practices.
