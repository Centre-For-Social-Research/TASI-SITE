TASI 2025

AI, Disinformation & Democratic Integrity

India's first national civil-society-led trust and safety convening. TASI 2025 reimagined what it means to build safer, more inclusive, and accountable digital environments in an era shaped by artificial intelligence and rapid technological change. Recognised as a pre-summit event for India's AI Impact Summit (February 2026).

  •   October 7–8, 2025 · New Delhi, India
  •   350+ Delegates · 100+ Speakers · 15 Countries
  •   30+ Sessions · Panels, Workshops, Fireside Chats, Roundtables
  •   Supported by MeitY, diplomatic missions, tech platforms
Key Strategic Outcomes:
  • Ecosystem Co-creation: Shifted industry engagement from compliance to active, cross-sector co-design of solutions.
  • India-First Innovation: Re-anchored global safety frameworks within the cultural and linguistic realities of the Global South.
  • Sustained Engagement: Forged lasting working groups across government, tech platforms, and civil society to build anticipatory digital governance models year-round.
