Strategic Focus

TASI 2026 Themes

TASI 2026 will convene leaders across government, industry, civil society and academia to examine the most urgent questions shaping AI governance and digital trust.

01

AI Governance and Safety

Building ethical, accountable, and inclusive AI frameworks rooted in transparency and global cooperation.

02

Child Protection

Designing safer digital environments for children, grounded in privacy and protection from online harms.

03

Youth Wellbeing

Supporting young people's healthy engagement with digital spaces, balancing participation, autonomy, and safety.

04

Gendered and Sexualized Harms Online

Addressing technology-facilitated gender-based violence (TFGBV) and emerging forms of digital exploitation.

05

Trust and Safety Workforce Well-being

Recognizing emotional labor in moderation teams and prioritizing mental health in digital governance ecosystems.

06

Safety by Design

Embedding safety and accountability into technologies from the outset of the design and deployment process.

07

Platform Responsibility and Cross-Sector Collaboration

Strengthening cooperation among governments, civil society, and platforms to address online harms systematically.

08

Scams & Fraud Mitigation

Combating digital fraud through cross-industry intelligence and rapid-response tracing mechanisms.

09

Digital Rights & India-first Innovation

Contextualizing global digital rights for the Global South and balancing free speech with protection against targeted abuse.
